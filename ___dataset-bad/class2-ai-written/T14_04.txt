Leaning back in her office chair, Anika stared at the final project timeline pinned to her wall. It was easy to see the chain of lucky coincidences that had landed it on her desk: her boss being on vacation, a server crash that delayed a rival team, a chance conversation in the elevator. But that wasn’t the whole story, was it? She remembered the nights spent sketching out solutions when the initial brief seemed impossible, the deliberate choice to ask for help from a colleague she barely knew, and the sheer persistence it took to get everyone in one room to agree. The opportunities might have just appeared, like random currents in a river, but she was the one who had built the rudder, caught the wind, and steered. Looking at the clear, achievable milestones ahead, she felt a quiet surge of confidence—not because the way forward was guaranteed, but because she knew she was capable of navigating whatever came next.
