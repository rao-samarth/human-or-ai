At the head table, Veronica was the picture of untouchable elegance. Her toast to the newlyweds was a masterpiece of wit and warmth, delivered in a voice that made champagne sound flat. Everyone watched, captivated, as she moved with a dancer's poise, her gown a column of shimmering silver. She was less a person and more a concept, a perfectly curated vision of success and sophistication that made you instinctively check if your own tie was straight. It was only when she gestured grandly towards the happy couple that the illusion cracked. Sticking to the bottom of her impossibly high stiletto, illuminated for a brief, glorious moment, was a large, slightly squashed piece of pink bubble gum. The goddess of the gala, it turned out, had stepped in something sticky on her way in, just like any other mortal.
