Master Gower, his face a very thundercloud of wrath, smote the ledger with his fist. "Why dost thou stand so, like a dumb statue, when I have given thee a plain command? This sum must be amended!" The young clerk, whose narrow shoulders bore no sigil of import, did not so much as flinch. "You bade me make a falsehood true with my own quill, sir," he said, his voice a quiet thing in that vast, silent chamber of accounts.
