My cousin suggested we just get up to change the channel, a piece of ancient wisdom met with theatrical groans from the digital natives on the floor. Just as true despair began to set in, Grandma, with a triumphant little "Aha!", produced it from the fruit bowl, nestled between the bananas and a suspiciously bruised apple. A wave of relieved laughter washed over the room.
