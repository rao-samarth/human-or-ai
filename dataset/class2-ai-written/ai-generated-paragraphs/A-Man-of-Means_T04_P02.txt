Ever since Gerald inherited his great-aunt’s obscure-but-shockingly-valuable porcelain cat empire, his social life has undergone a radical transformation. Tonight, at this charity gala, he’s less a person and more a human magnet for everyone with a "can't-miss" investment opportunity. A man I’m sure he’s never met is passionately pitching a start-up for bespoke shoelaces, while a woman in a dazzling sequin dress keeps trying to recall a shared preschool experience that almost certainly never happened. His jokes, which last year were met with polite silence, now earn roaring laughter that seems to shake the champagne flutes. You can practically see the mental calculations happening behind their eager smiles, each one hoping to become the favorite in this sudden, undeclared competition for his attention and, more importantly, his checkbook.
