Yet the true victory wasn't found in the final score. It was in the hand that helped a fallen player to their feet, a gesture quicker than any fast break. It was in the shared, breathless laugh after an impossible play, a moment of mutual admiration that outshone the streetlights. Here, they weren't just trying to beat one another; they were pushing each other higher, building a quiet, resilient bond forged in the heat of a game that meant more than winning.
