You keep saying I lost my mind, but what if I just lost the mind you were comfortable with? The one that kept quiet and played by the rules? You can’t imagine the clarity that comes when everything you believe in shatters into a million pieces. It’s terrifying, yes. For a while, I saw only the jagged edges, the horrifying sharpness of it all. But then you start to see the light shining *through* the cracks. You see the machinery behind the stage, the strings on all the puppets, including your own. It’s not madness to finally see the truth of the whole awful, beautiful system. The real madness is what you’re doing right now: carefully, deliberately, choosing not to look.
