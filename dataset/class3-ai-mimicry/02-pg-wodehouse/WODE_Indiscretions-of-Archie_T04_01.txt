Algernon,” Penelope began, her voice having acquired that specific, arctic crispness a fellow might associate with a judge about to hand down a life sentence for pinching a policeman’s helmet, “explain it to me again. You felt that my Uncle Roderick’s pet parrot, a bird known for its sensitive disposition and frankly shocking vocabulary, would benefit from a change of scenery. This, in your considered view, necessitated relocating his cage directly beside the open window of the nursery, where the Pringle twins were engaged in what you charmingly termed ‘a spot of target practice’ with their new air rifles. I only ask because Uncle Roderick is currently attempting to coax the traumatised creature down from the weather vane with a bag of walnuts and a fishing net, while the Pringle twins are explaining to their Nanny precisely where they learned the phrase ‘you blithering catawampus’. The whole enterprise, you see, has the distinct flavour of a scheme hatched by a half-witted stoat.
