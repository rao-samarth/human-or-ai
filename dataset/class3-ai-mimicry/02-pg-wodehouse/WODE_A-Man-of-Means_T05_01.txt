The scenery chugging past the window was all very well, I suppose, if you’re the sort of cove who goes in for cows and whatnot, but my mind was otherwise engaged, wrestling as I was with the contents of Biffy’s latest communiqué. It seems the poor blighter has convinced himself that the world is crying out for parakeets capable of quoting Aeschylus. He’d acquired a flock of the little green perishers and was spending his days, so he wrote, attempting to drum the basics of classical tragedy into their tiny, seed-obsessed brains.
