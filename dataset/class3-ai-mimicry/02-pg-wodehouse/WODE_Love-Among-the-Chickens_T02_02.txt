Don't just stand there wobbling like a blancmange, old man, this requires a bit of snap!" I hissed, yanking him back behind the privet hedge. "The core of the problem, you see, remains her blasted Uncle Sebastian, a man whose sole purpose in life appears to be guarding the girl as if she were the last pot of marmalade in a besieged garrison. He has expressly forbidden my presence on the grounds, which, while a dashed nuisance, is hardly the end of the world. He anticipates a frontal assault, a polite ring at the bell, perhaps a calling card. What the blighter won't anticipate is a diversionary tactic involving Mrs. Higgins's prize-winning Pekinese and a strategically lobbed tennis ball, giving me the thirty seconds I need to scale the trellis to her balcony. It's the sheer, unexpected velocity of the enterprise that will carry the day! Now, brace yourself and prepare to whistle when you see the dog.
