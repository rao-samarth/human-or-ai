Their adoration is a summer's torrent, full of noise and fury, that by eventide becomes a shallow, muddy trickle, leaving naught behind but wreckage and regret. Better, I think, to be hated from the first than to be so loved, for their favour is a poison sweetened for a moment, and its end is ever bitter.
