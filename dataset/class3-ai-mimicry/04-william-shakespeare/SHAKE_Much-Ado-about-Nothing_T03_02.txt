Do you mark him yonder, my lady, how he stands apart from the revels? That is the Baron of Estwick, whose name but a fortnight past was praised in every hall.” The elder countess gestured with her fan, her voice a low murmur beneath the music's swell. “Now see how even the serving-men do slight him. They say a rival, envious of his ascent, let fly a single, venomous word into the ear of the Prince—a mere invention, a spiteful fancy—yet it took root like a pernicious weed. For what defense hath a man against a shadow? His whole life's virtue, his brave deeds in the field, all are turned to naught and made suspect by one unseen whisper. Truly, the estimation of the world is a most fragile and glassy thing, shattered as easily by a lie as by a stone.
