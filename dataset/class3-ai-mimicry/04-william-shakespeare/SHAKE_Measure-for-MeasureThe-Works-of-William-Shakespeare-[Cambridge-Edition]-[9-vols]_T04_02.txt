They offered him the iron circlet, that same crown his father wore, a heavy thing that promised dominion unchecked and a will that none might question. The courtiers watched, their faces masks of expectation, for they knew how such a weight could press upon the soul, breeding cruelty from the seed of command. Yet he did not reach for it.
