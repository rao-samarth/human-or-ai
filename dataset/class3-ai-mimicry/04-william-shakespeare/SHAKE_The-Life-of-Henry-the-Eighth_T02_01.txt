But the King’s own law is plain, sir,” the young man protested, adjusting his cloak against the biting wind that swept across the moor. “Lord Faxton stands accused by a dozen mouths; how can the court do aught but find him guilty?” His master, a grey and weary man, offered a short, humourless laugh that was snatched away by the gale. “Thou speakest of law as a thing of stone, my boy, when it is mere clay, to be shaped by the strongest hands. Think you that a lord’s fat purse carries less weight than the thin pleas of common folk? Nay, forsooth! His coin shall sing a sweeter tune to the magistrate’s ear than any chorus of witnesses. Justice is a blind-folded lady, 'tis true, but she may yet feel the heft of gold placed in her palm, and her scales, I promise you, are quick to tip for those who know how to press upon them. By nightfall, his lordship shall be free, and the truth left shivering in the cold, a beggar at its own feast.
