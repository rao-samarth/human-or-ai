Behold this humble town of ours, where the market square rings with honest laughter and not with the clatter of an enforcer's guard. No longer do men speak in fearful whispers, for the sceptre that once did fall like a cudgel, crushing the spirit and seizing the coin, is now held as a guiding staff. The law, which erewhile was a net to ensnare the simple man, hath been fashioned into a fortress for his protection.
