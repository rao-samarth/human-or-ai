The old man waved a dismissive hand, the great rings upon it glinting in the candlelight. A poor man, caught in its folds, is smothered for his pains, whilst a man of substance may turn and twist the garment to his liking, making of its solemn edicts a shield against his foes or a mantle for his own ambition.
