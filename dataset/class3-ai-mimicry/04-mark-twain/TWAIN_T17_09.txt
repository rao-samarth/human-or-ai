Now, a sensible man might have sought clarification, but pride is a more potent director of human affairs than sense will ever be; and so each man, convinced of his own righteous interpretation, set to work. They proceeded to erect two entirely different commercial enterprises under the same roof, each believing the other to be engaged in a kind of deliberate, malicious idiocy. The result was not a disagreement, but a demolition, and a partnership of two decades was reduced to little more than a pile of lawsuits and mutual contempt, all fertilized by a half-dozen ill-chosen words on a sheet of company letterhead.
