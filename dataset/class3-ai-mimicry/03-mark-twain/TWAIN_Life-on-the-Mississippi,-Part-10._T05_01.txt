I am here to tell you the plain truth of it, for it’s a thing that has been puffed up with a great deal of nonsense. For years you watch the old man, and you convince yourself he’s got some private understanding with the Almighty concerning the proper angle of a joint or the temper of a blade. You follow his rules like they was scripture, thinking every grunt and squint is part of a secret ceremony; meanwhile, your own hands feel like a pair of borrowed shovels.
