I do wish you were here, my friend, for this hotel lobby furnishes more theater than any proper stage I know. Just yesterday, I overheard a gentleman of considerable girth and even more considerable volume lay out his plans for a fishing trip that would have humbled the apostles themselves. He spoke of leviathans lurking in the local stream, of battles of wit and sinew against legendary trout, and of a grand fish-fry to follow that would feed the entire county. His pronouncements were so thunderous that a sleeping dog two chairs down yelped and fled the scene.
