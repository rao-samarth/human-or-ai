To the gentlemen at that adjacent table, I am merely a fellow diner enjoying his port; they cannot conceive that the man beside them holds a piece of knowledge that, were it to slip from his lips, could set the telegraphs of Europe ablaze and send armies marching before breakfast. It is a damnable position, to know that the tranquility of an entire continent rests upon one's discretion, and yet, what can one do?
