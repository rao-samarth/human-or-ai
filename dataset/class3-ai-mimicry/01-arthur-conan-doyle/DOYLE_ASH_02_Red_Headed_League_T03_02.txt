The rhythmic clatter of the carriage wheels upon the track seems to echo the frantic beating of your own heart, for I perceive you are still quite pale from the affair at the priory. You speak of phantoms, of some unholy visitation, and allow your imagination to be consumed by the most lurid and impossible of explanations. Yet I implore you, turn your mind from such fevered fancies! The true horror of this world is not to be found in the ethereal mist of a graveyard; it lies, I assure you, within the cunning and desperate heart of man.
