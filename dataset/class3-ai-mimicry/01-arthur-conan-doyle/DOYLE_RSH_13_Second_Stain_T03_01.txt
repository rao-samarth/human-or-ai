The crumpled note in his greatcoat felt heavier than a block of lead, its vile contents a poison that had already begun its insidious work upon his soul. To acquiesce was to chain himself to a life of perpetual servitude, his fortune and good name bled away by degrees until nothing whatever remained of the man he had been.
