Though the constable found clear evidence of a most desperate and furious struggle, his inquiries were met with a wall of deliberate ignorance. It seemed to the more philosophical mind that such a conclusion was not a matter of chance, but rather the final balancing of a long and bloody ledger; a life of brutal imposition had simply, and at last, been met with an equally brutal reply.
