You have, sir, a most singular talent for the dramatic, but your performance upon this occasion has been found wanting,” he remarked, his voice carrying with perfect clarity across the hushed dining room of the club. His companion, a portly fellow with a florid complexion, sputtered into his claret. “The entire affair, from the forged promissory note to the conveniently discovered locket, bears the unmistakable stamp of your own peculiar genius for malevolence. Did you truly imagine that such a clumsy contrivance—a web of lies so transparently woven—could condemn a man of impeccable character whilst you, the true architect of this villainy, retired to enjoy his fortune? It was a diabolical scheme, I grant you, yet your vanity has proven your undoing. You left your own monogrammed handkerchief near the safe, a piece of audacious folly that has now brought your whole contemptible artifice crashing down. Observe his pallor, gentlemen; the truth appears to have a most unwholesome effect upon his constitution.
