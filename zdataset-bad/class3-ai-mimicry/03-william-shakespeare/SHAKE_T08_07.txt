He calls it the King's own justice, what was done to my father's house, yet I saw no scales nor blindfolded maid, only the glint of coin in a corrupt palm. They speak of right, these men in their fur-trimmed gowns, and bid us be content with heaven's slow accounting; but what comfort is a future paradise when the present world is a wilderness of thieves? Shall I pray for his soul whilst he sups upon my inheritance and warms himself at my hearth? A pox on such patience! There is a plainer truth, a more immediate sentence to be writ, not by a clerk's dull quill upon some dusty parchment, but with a sharper point upon the villain's very flesh. Let them keep their courts and their ancient precedents; my heart holds a different ledger, and its debts shall be paid in a currency they cannot counterfeit.
