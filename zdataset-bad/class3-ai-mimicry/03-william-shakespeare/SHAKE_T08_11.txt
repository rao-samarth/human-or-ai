Hark how my brother puffeth out his chest, his belly full of capon and his tongue full of righteous fire. He speaks of the slight done to our house, and of the swift account he rendered unto our foe, and all his mewling kin do nod and smile as if Solon himself had descended to pass judgement. A fair reckoning, he calls it; a balancing of the books. Yet I see well the truth of it: he hath taken a pound of flesh for a stolen penny, and called the scales even.
