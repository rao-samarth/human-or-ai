“You will cease these paltry equivocations at once, Mr. Finch!” boomed the Admiral, his shadow thrown vast and menacing by the single lamp upon the desk. “Do you take me for a fool? The talk has reached my ears from a dozen quarters—subtle at first, a mere whisper on the wind, but a gale of insinuation now. This is no longer a private matter of finance between us; you have made it an affair of public character, and by extension, you have sullied mine. A man’s standing, sir, is the only currency of true worth he possesses, and you have gambled with it as though it were a common shilling! There is but one path from this precipice, and it is a steep and costly one. You will make full restitution
