It was a trifling sum, Abernathy, a mere pittance against the whole of your estate,” murmured the stout man, his hand resting upon his companion's slumped shoulder. The other, a gaunt figure whose face seemed etched with the very lines of his misfortune, shook his head slowly, his gaze fixed upon the rain-streaked window. "You misapprehend the matter entirely, Croft," he replied, his voice a dry whisper that scarcely carried beyond their table, yet was perfectly audible in the room’s profound stillness. "The currency was of no consequence; a hundred pounds or a hundred thousand, it would have been the same. It was the imputation, you see—the shadow of a doubt cast upon a name that three generations have held stainless. I have paid the price to see that shadow banished, and though it leaves me a pauper in all but spirit, I should consider it a bargain were it to cost me my very last breath.
