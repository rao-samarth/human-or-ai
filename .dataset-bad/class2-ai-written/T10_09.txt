Priya spent a week wrestling with the project files, confused by the gaps in the client history. The official summaries she’d been given painted a picture of a difficult, demanding partner, making her team hesitant and defensive. But out of sheer frustration, she started digging into the raw server archives late one night. What she found were not just the complaints, but also the praises, the detailed positive feedback, the context that had been systematically stripped away. The next morning, instead of presenting another cautious strategy, she shared her screen and walked everyone through the complete communication log. A silence fell over the room, followed by a slow, collective exhale. Suddenly, the client wasn't an adversary to be managed, but a person to be understood. The project’s entire energy shifted, not with a dramatic announcement, but with the quiet, optimistic power of a story finally told in its entirety.
