The house is still as death's own chamber, yet my lord's kind words do echo still, a thunder in this quiet. He calls me kinsman, speaks of lands and futures built on what? On air, on shadows, on a name I stole from some poor wretch's grave. To speak the truth now were to prove myself a villain twofold: first for the lie, and then for the cruel undoing of his present joy.
