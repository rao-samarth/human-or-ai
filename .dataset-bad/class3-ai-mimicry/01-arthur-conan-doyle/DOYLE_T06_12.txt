My friend, a man whose usual placid nature was seldom disturbed, suddenly gripped my arm with a surprising force. “Look there,” he muttered, his voice low and tight, nodding towards the haberdashery opposite. “See how Mr Abernathy preens in his new-found prosperity.” I followed his gaze and perceived a portly fellow, until recently a man of no particular account, now sporting a waistcoat of a most audacious silk and swinging a silver-topped cane with an air of immense self-satisfaction.
