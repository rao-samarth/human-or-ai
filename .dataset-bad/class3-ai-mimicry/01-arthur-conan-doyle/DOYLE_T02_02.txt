It was a most singular and, I must confess, heartening thing to witness young Abernathy during the quarterly audit. Hitherto, he had presented to the firm a meticulously crafted persona of the meekest sort—a man of shuffling gait and downcast eyes, whose contributions were ever whispered with a profound apology. Yet, when the senior partner’s figures were found to be in a hopeless muddle, this quiet fellow underwent a startling metamorphosis. The feigned timidity fell away like a discarded coat, revealing a mind of remarkable precision and an astonishing command of the company’s finances. With a quiet authority that none suspected he possessed, he unravelled the entire knotted affair in under an hour. It became manifest to all that the man had not been hiding a deficiency, but rather a brilliance, and in its final, glorious emergence, our collective future suddenly appeared a great deal more secure.
