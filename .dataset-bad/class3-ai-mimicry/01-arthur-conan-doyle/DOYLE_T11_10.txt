The man’s fortune is a thing of recent construction, and like all such structures, it quite lacks a solid foundation. Did you not observe his manner with the footman? It was not the easy, almost unconscious, command of one born to service, but the sharp, impatient tone of a man who has only lately learned to pay for it. There exists, you see, an entire unspoken grammar of conduct which is not taught in schools but is absorbed, as if from the very air, from one’s infancy.
