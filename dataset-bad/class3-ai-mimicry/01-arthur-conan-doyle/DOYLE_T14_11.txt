Here was a ticket stub from a Parisian theatre; beside it, a shard of blue porcelain from a shattered vase; and further still, a single, tarnished brass button. It is a most common error to view such a collection as the mere debris of chance, to believe that these items were simply washed ashore by the tides of circumstance.
