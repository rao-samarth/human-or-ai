And what was this noble spirit, this sworn foe of commerce, doing amidst the temples of retail? He was, if you please, engaged in a life-or-death struggle with a shop assistant over the last available pair of lavender-hued spats, his face a thunderous crimson that clashed violently with the object of his desire. His arguments against the hollow pursuit of earthly trinkets, it seemed, did not extend to fashionable ankle-wear.
