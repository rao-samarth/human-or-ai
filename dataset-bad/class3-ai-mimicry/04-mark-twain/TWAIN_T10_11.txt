You served me up a fine and particular selection of facts, Judge, like a man picking only the shiniest apples from a barrel that’s half-rotten clear through to the bottom. Don’t you look at me with that benevolent pity; I am not a schoolboy to be mollified by a pat on the head. You told me what the man did on Tuesday, and what he said on Friday—and every word of it was gospel. But the whole miserable affair hinged on a Wednesday, a day you skipped over so neatly it might as well have been plucked from the calendar by God Almighty himself.
