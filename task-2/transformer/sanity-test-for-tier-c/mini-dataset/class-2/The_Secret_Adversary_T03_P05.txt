Anya’s hand hovered over the ‘send’ button, the cursor a tiny, rhythmic heartbeat on the screen. The attached folder contained everything: falsified safety reports, damning internal memos, the names of everyone involved. Sending it to the regulators would mean the end of her career at Sterling Corp—likely the end of it anywhere in this industry. They would sue her, slander her, make an absolute example of her. An icy knot of fear tightened in her stomach, a physical weight pulling her down. But then she pictured the faces of families who would use the faulty equipment, completely unaware of the danger. Her own comfort suddenly felt impossibly small compared to that potential harm. The fear didn't vanish, but a different feeling rose to meet it—a quiet, steely resolve. It wasn't a choice anymore. It was simply what had to be done. Her finger pressed down. Click.
