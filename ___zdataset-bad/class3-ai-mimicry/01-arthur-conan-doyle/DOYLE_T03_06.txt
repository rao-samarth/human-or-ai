But what can he truly comprehend of the decades of unblemished service, the careful and arduous construction of a life's work, which this sordid imputation now threatens to bring crashing down into rubble? He perceives a simple transaction, a momentary indiscretion, whilst I see the yawning abyss. A single word of this calumny whispered abroad would undo everything, leaving only disgrace.
