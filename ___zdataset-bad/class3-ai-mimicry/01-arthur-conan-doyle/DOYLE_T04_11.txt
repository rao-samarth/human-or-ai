Your hand hesitates over the ledger, Davies, and the figures you have entered this past fortnight show a singular lack of your usual precision. It is not, I think, a matter of simple fatigue; indeed, the rumours of a most generous proposition put to you by Albright & Sons have not escaped my notice. They offer a glittering purse, I have no doubt, in the hopes of purchasing not only your skill, but the very confidences of your current houseâ€”a common enough stratagem in the City. Yet I put it to you that a connection forged through years of mutual effort and shared adversity possesses a substance which no sudden quantity of coin can replicate. Such a bond, when sundered for the mere convenience of a higher wage, is a thing that can never be wholly mended. Consider, my boy, which of these currencies will hold the greater value when the final accounting is made.
