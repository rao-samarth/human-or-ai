My companion, a man whose life had heretofore been a study in cautious deliberation, was granted but a single heartbeat. In that infinitesimal pause, one might have conjectured upon the great forces which had brought these two men together—a chain of seemingly trivial events, a conspiracy of chance, now culminating in this crude and violent tableau. Yet, what I saw in his face was not the placid resignation of a pawn moved by an unseen hand. Instead, a new and terrible light of pure resolve ignited in his eyes.
