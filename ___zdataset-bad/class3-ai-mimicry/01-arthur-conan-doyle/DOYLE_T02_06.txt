As the green shires of the countryside sped by the carriage window, my attention was drawn to the gentleman opposite, who held forth upon matters of distant skirmishes with an air of immense, albeit borrowed, authority. He had the bronzed countenance and the clipped moustache of the campaigning man, to be sure; yet, it was in the trifling details that the entire artifice threatened to unravel. His hands, for example, were those of a city clerk, uncalloused and soft, and when he gestured to illustrate some point of grand strategy, his cuff revealed immaculate linen quite at odds with the rugged persona.
